Linus Torvalds

Linus Torvalds is a Finnish-American software engineer best known for creating the Linux operating system kernel. He initiated the development of Linux in 1991, which has since become a cornerstone of open-source software. Torvalds is also known for his work on Git, a version control system that has transformed software development.
