Musical Instruments

Music is a form of artistic expression that connects people and enriches our lives.

Guitar

The guitar is a string instrument played with fingers or a pick.

  • Type: String
  • Main material: Wood
  • Common use: Solo, accompaniment

Piano

The piano is a keyboard instrument that produces sound by striking strings with hammers.

  • Type: String
  • Main material: Wood and metal
  • Common use: Solo, accompaniment

Drums

The drums are a percussion instrument played by striking drums and cymbals.

  • Type: Percussion
  • Main material: Wood and metal
  • Common use: Accompaniment
