Planets of the Solar System

Mars

Mars is known as the red planet due to its characteristic color.

  • Approximate distance from the sun: 227.9 million km
  • Rotation time: 24.6 hours
  • Number of moons: 2
  • Presence of atmosphere: Yes

Jupiter

Jupiter is the largest planet in the solar system and is known for its cloud bands.

  • Approximate distance from the sun: 778.5 million km
  • Rotation time: 9.9 hours
  • Number of moons: 79
  • Presence of atmosphere: Yes

Saturn

Saturn is famous for its impressive rings and is the second largest planet.

  • Approximate distance from the sun: 1.4 billion km
  • Rotation time: 10.7 hours
  • Number of moons: 83
  • Presence of atmosphere: Yes
