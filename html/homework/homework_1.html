Social Networks

Social networks are platforms that allow users to connect, share content, and communicate with each other. They have become an integral part of modern society, influencing how we interact and share information.

Social Network Number of Users Creation Year link
Facebook 3 billion 2004 Facebook
WhatsApp 3 billion 2009 WhatsApp
Instagram 3 billion 2010 Instagram
YouTube 2.58 billion 2003 YouTube
TikTok 1.9 billion 2016 TikTok

Social networks continue to shape global communication and commerce, connecting billions of people across geographical boundaries. Their influence extends to politics, business, and culture, making them one of the most significant technological developments of the 21st century.
